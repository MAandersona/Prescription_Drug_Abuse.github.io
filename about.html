About Us

We are a group of students

We made this website as our outcome for our Global Perspectives Team Project, the goal of this website is to spread awareness of the effect of prescription drug abuse Globaly and in rural and urban areas. Prescription drug abuse tends to be overlooked and overshadowed by plain drug abuse despite it being as, if not more dangerous than ordinary drug abuse. Acetaminophen (paracetamol) toxicity is the second most common cause of liver transplantation worldwide (Suneil Agrawal; Babak Khazaeni 2022). According to the United Nations Office on Drugs and Crime apart from a few studies reporting the prevalence and patterns of non-medical use of prescription drugs, limited data is available on drug use from many regions of the world. This website is our attempt to change that.
